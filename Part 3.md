&#x20;  **Attention and Transformer Reflection**



i) Why RNNs struggle with long-term dependencies:

&#x20;  RNNs process data sequentially, so they often forget earlier information when sequences become long. This causes the vanishing gradient problem.



ii) How LSTMs help with memory:

&#x20;   LSTMs use memory cells and gates to store important information for longer periods, helping the model remember previous context.



iii) What attention solves in sequence-to-sequence tasks:

&#x20;    Attention allows the model to focus on the most relevant words in the input sequence instead of depending only on the previous hidden state.



iv) Why transformers are important in modern NLP and Generative AI:

&#x20;   Transformers process data in parallel and use attention mechanisms, making them faster and more accurate. They are the foundation of modern AI models like ChatGPT, BERT, and Gemini.
